About

Since our beginnings in 1911, our mission has always been, and will always be, to reach out to those around us in Christ-like ways, making Christlike disciples in the Kansas City area, and around the world. We have found that through intentionally orienting ourselves around four ways of being Christ's followers, we can most effectively carry out that mission: worship: the daily surrender of our lives to Christ in adoration and thankfulness for what he has done for us; community: building relationships with one another as the body of Christ, opening our hands and hearts to those inside and outside our local church; discipleship: being intentional about making the most of every opportunity to know more about Christ and what it looks like to follow Him; and compassion: going into our world with the love of Christ.

Vision

At Atlantis Church of the Nazarene, we desire to follow the example of Christ. We love God, love others, and want to facilitate change for the good in our community. So we love Worshipping God, we make it our goal to love others and our aim is to serve our community.
